Kutti Card Game

A Strategic Card Game

📜 How to Play

  • Phase 1 — Kutti Distribution: Cards are drawn one at a time for each player.
  • If a drawn card is exactly 1 rank higher than another player's card, the lower card transfers to the higher card's player!
  • Phase 2 — Trick Playing: Player with ♠Q starts. Play any card from your hand.
  • Highest rank wins the trick (no suit restriction) & collects all cards.
  • 🏆 First player to empty their hand WINS!
  • 🐕 Last player holding cards is the Dock (Dog)!
