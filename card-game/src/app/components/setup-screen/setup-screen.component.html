Kutti Card Game

A Trick-Taking Card Game

📜 How to Play

  • 52 cards dealt equally among all players
  • Player with the highest ♠ Spade starts the game
  • You must follow the lead suit if you have it
  • Highest card of the lead suit wins the trick & collects all cards
  • 🏆 Player with fewest collected cards wins!
  • 🐕 Player with most collected cards is the Dog!
